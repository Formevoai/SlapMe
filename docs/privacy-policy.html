SlapMe — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

SlapMe ("we", "our", or "the app") is a novelty entertainment app that plays sound effects when you slap your phone. Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

1. Data We Collect

Motion sensor data: SlapMe uses your device's accelerometer (via CoreMotion) to detect impacts in real time. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted, stored, or shared.

No personal data: We do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

No analytics or tracking: SlapMe does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or third-party trackers.

2. In-App Purchases

SlapMe offers a one-time premium upgrade through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

3. Data Storage

Your app preferences (selected sound pack, sensitivity, volume settings) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not synced to any server.

4. Third-Party Services

SlapMe does not use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs. The app works fully offline.

5. Children's Privacy

SlapMe does not knowingly collect any data from children. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the app is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: slapmeapp@proton.me
